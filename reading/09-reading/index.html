Materials from class on Monday, July 19, 2021

Communicating with ggplot2

  • BBC, BBC Visual and Data Journalism cookbook for R graphics (2019).
  • K. Healy, Data Visualization: A Practical Introduction (Princeton: Princeton University Press, 2018).
    – Chapter 8
    Free online.
  • H. Wickham and G. Grolemund, R for Data Science: Import, Tidy, Transform, Visualize, and Model Data (Sebastopol, California: O’Reilly Media, 2017).
    – Chapter 28
    Free online.

Visual storytelling

  • R. Roth, “Cartographic Design as Visual Storytelling: Synthesis and Review of Map-Based Narratives, Genres, and Tropes,” The Cartographic Journal 0, no. 0 (2020): 1–32.
  • N. Henry-Riche et al., eds., in Data-Driven Storytelling (Boca Raton, 2018).

Covid-19 visual analysis and communication

  • Y. Zhang et al., “Mapping the Landscape of COVID-19 Crisis Visualizations,” CoRR abs/2101.04743 (2021).
